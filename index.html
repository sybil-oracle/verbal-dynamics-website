How We Work

Our process is structured, iterative, and gate-driven — each stage creates explicit artifacts and verified outcomes.

1) Define: clarify goals, constraints, and success criteria.
2) Specify: write implementation plans and test requirements.
3) Build: execute with specialized AI coding agents.
4) Verify: independent checks against requirements and test cases.
5) Deploy: release to infrastructure and monitor behavior.
6) Iterate: feed findings back into the next delivery cycle.
